Prague Castle Escape is a mobile game developed as a free app by the developer for fun. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and protected when you use the app.

1. Information Collection and Use

The app collects location data to enable outdoor gameplay experiences that depend on your real-world position. Your location is used only while the game is active and is not stored after the game ends.

At the end of the game, the app asks you to enter a name or nickname of your choice. This name, along with your game result (e.g., score or completion status), is saved in a secure Firebase Firestore database. This allows you to view your results later or compare them with others.

We do not collect any other personally identifiable information such as email addresses, phone numbers, or contact lists.

2. Data Sharing

We do not share any of your data with third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your location is not stored or logged after the game session ends. Your result and the name you provide are stored in Firebase Firestore, a secure cloud database hosted by Google.

4. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your data. All stored data is secured using Firebase's built-in security rules and access controls.
